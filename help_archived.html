Get in touch! We're here to help you with your Guides.

Contact Us

There are several ways to reach us, depending on your needs. We recommend the following ways to get in touch:

  • Read the FAQ—perhaps your question has been asked before.
  • Open a chat window, below, for answers to quick questions.
  • Open a ticket in ServiceNow to report more serious issues.
  • Contact any LMT member individually or through our listserv at lmt-list@nd.edu.

Chat with a Trainer

If no trainers are online and available, you may email us or open a ticket in Service Now.
