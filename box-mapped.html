Mapped Box

Certain boxes are commonly shared among Guide authors. By mapping to these relatively static boxes rather than copying them, we can reduce workload and ensure changes to content are propagated to all guides when the source box is changed. Mapped boxes are stored in the Reusable Assets Guide and include:

  • Books
  • Theses and Dissertations
  • Specific Search Widgets
  • Citation Managers
  • Citation Styles

Don't Map to Your Own Guides
We DO NOT recommend mapping to boxes in your own guides. This web of mapping relationships becomes complex and difficult to remember. And, as guide authors regularly unpublish, archive and delete guides, content may be inadvertently deleted from guides dependent on mapped content. Instead, request a mapped box in our Reusable Assets guide!
